Transfer ownership

Enter the email address of the account you want to transfer ownership to. They must have an existing Relay account.

Deactivate account

Deactivating your account will:

  • Hide your profile from other users
  • Pause all active projects and deployments
  • Suspend any API keys and integrations

Your data will be preserved. You can reactivate at any time by signing in again.

Delete account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Deleting your account will immediately:

  • Remove all projects, deployments, and data
  • Cancel any active subscriptions
  • Revoke all API keys and integrations
  • Remove you from all teams
